Prospective Students

Thank you for your interest in our research at the University of Michigan. I welcome all emails and I read them — if you have a website and a Google Scholar entry, please share them.

Graduate Students

If you are outside U-M, please apply to the U-M graduate program this Fall; financial-aid decisions are made in the Spring. If you are a master's student at U-M, consider taking one of my classes or joining the group as an intern.

When emailing, include a CV/resume, an unofficial transcript, your website, and a Google Scholar entry if you have publications.

Undergraduate Students

If you are a U-M undergraduate, consider taking one of my classes or joining the group through UROP / an internship. I welcome any chance to chat in person.

When emailing, include a CV/resume and an unofficial transcript.
